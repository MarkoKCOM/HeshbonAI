## 1) What HeshbonAI is

### Short version
HeshbonAI is a white-label AI assistant for CPA firms.

It automates client communication, follow-up, document collection, question handling, and workflow coordination through WhatsApp and SMS.

### One-line pitch
We help CPA firms stop wasting hours chasing clients for documents and routine replies by putting a firm-branded AI assistant in front of that workflow.

### What to emphasize
- White-label - fully branded to the CPA firm
- Clients think they are talking to a real team member
- Works through WhatsApp and SMS
- Follows up automatically until documents arrive
- Reads, scans, and analyzes incoming PDFs and files
- Answers routine client questions 24/7
- Keeps status, missing items, and deadlines organized
- Syncs with QuickBooks, Google Drive, Dropbox, Google Calendar, Outlook, and Zapier

### What NOT to say
- Do not call it a bot
- Do not say quick setup / 15-minute setup / no IT needed
- Do not promise features that do not exist
- Do not promise fixed USA pricing unless approved

## 2) Core pain points to lead with

Use the pain first. That is what gets attention.

- Your staff is spending hours every week reminding clients, checking who replied, and following up again
- Skilled staff is doing admin instead of billable work
- Deadlines get stressful because documents come in late
- Client communication is scattered across email, text, calls, and WhatsApp
- Clients ignore portals and email reminders but respond to messages on their phone
- Staff keeps answering the same questions again and again

Best framing:
"Most firms do not have a document problem. They have a follow-up problem."

## 6) Discovery questions

Do not jump straight into pitching. Get them talking.

### Good discovery questions
- How are you currently collecting documents from clients?
- Is that mostly email, phone, portal, text, or WhatsApp today?
- How much staff time goes into reminders and follow-up each week?
- Who on the team usually owns that process?
- What usually causes the biggest delays - no response, incomplete files, wrong files, or late submissions?
- Are clients asking the same routine questions over and over?
- Do you already use a portal or practice management platform?
- Do your clients respond better to text / WhatsApp than email?
- Is this more of a tax-season pain or year-round pain?
- If you could remove one bottleneck from the client communication side, what would it be?

### What you want to uncover
- Manual follow-up burden
- Late documents
- Staff time lost
- Poor response rates by email/portal
- Existing tech stack
- Whether decision-maker cares more about time, deadlines, client experience, or growth

## 9) Objection handling / Q&A

### Objection: "We already use email / a portal"
That makes sense.
Most firms we speak with already have some process in place.
The issue is usually not whether a process exists - it is whether clients actually respond fast enough and whether your staff still has to keep chasing.

HeshbonAI is useful when the current process still depends on manual follow-up, scattered replies, and routine back-and-forth.

### Objection: "Our clients are older. They do not use WhatsApp"
Understood.
That is exactly why SMS matters too.
For firms with clients who prefer text, HeshbonAI can support communication there as well.

The bigger point is meeting clients where they already respond instead of forcing them into a portal habit.

### Objection: "We already text clients manually"
Right - and that usually works better than email.
What HeshbonAI changes is that your team does not have to keep doing it manually.

It automates the reminders, tracks what is still missing, answers routine questions, and keeps the workflow organized.

### Objection: "How is this different from TaxDome / Liscio / Canopy?"
Those platforms are broader practice management or portal tools.
HeshbonAI is focused on the client-facing workflow that usually still stays manual - follow-up, document chasing, routine client communication, and keeping that flow moving through WhatsApp and SMS.

So it is less about being another portal and more about removing the follow-up burden from staff.

### Objection: "We do not want AI talking to clients"
Totally fair.
That usually comes from bad experiences with generic chat tools.

HeshbonAI is designed as a firm-branded AI assistant under your firm's identity. The point is not to sound robotic. The point is to handle routine communication clearly, professionally, and consistently while your team stays in control.

### Objection: "What kinds of questions can it answer?"
Routine client questions like:
- Did you get my documents?
- What is still missing?
- What is the deadline?
- Where do I send this?
- Was this received?

That removes a lot of repetitive back-and-forth from your team.

### Objection: "What happens when clients send the wrong file or an incomplete file?"
HeshbonAI reads, scans, and analyzes incoming PDFs and files. If something is wrong or incomplete, it can flag that and keep the process moving instead of letting the issue sit unnoticed.

### Objection: "Does it only do document collection?"
No.
That is usually the entry point, but it is broader than that.

It automates client communication, reminders, question handling, workflow coordination, and status tracking - not just document requests.

### Objection: "Will clients know it is software?"
The experience is branded to the firm and designed to feel like a real team member handling the communication.
That is a big part of why firms like it - it feels personal without requiring staff to do everything manually.

### Objection: "Does it integrate with our tools?"
Yes - the integrations we talk about today are QuickBooks, Google Drive, Dropbox, Google Calendar, Outlook, and Zapier.

### Objection: "How much does it cost?"
Pricing depends on the firm's size, workflow, and use case.

The best thing is to scope that after a short conversation once we understand volume and needs.

### Objection: "How long does it take to get running?"
The fastest way to answer that is on a short walkthrough once we understand how your current workflow works.
I do not want to guess before seeing your use case.

### Objection: "We are too small"
Small firms are often the ones that feel this pain the most because skilled people are wearing too many hats.
If your staff is losing time to repeated follow-up and routine replies, that is exactly where HeshbonAI can help.

### Objection: "We are too big / too complex"
That is actually where workflow coordination becomes even more valuable.
When more staff, more clients, and more deadlines are involved, manual follow-up gets expensive fast.

### Objection: "Send me something"
Happy to.
I will send over a short overview.
Usually the best next step after that is still a quick demo so you can see whether it fits your workflow.
